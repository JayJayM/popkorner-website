Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open Popkorner, go to your profile, tap the gear icon, then "Account Settings", and select "Delete Account". This permanently deletes all your data including reviews, watchlist, and profile photo.

Where does the movie data come from?

Movie information (titles, posters, descriptions, release dates) is provided by TMDB (The Movie Database). If you notice incorrect movie data, you can help improve it on TMDB's website.

Can I change my username or profile picture?

Yes. Go to your profile and tap the edit button to update your display name or profile photo at any time.

Is Popkorner free?

Yes, Popkorner is free to use. There are no ads and no premium subscription.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at support@popkorner.app with a description of what happened, what you expected, and your device model and iOS version. Screenshots help too.
