About Popkorner

One line: Discover what's playing, track what's coming, save your picks, see what your friends loved, and share your reviews beautifully.

Short: Popkorner is an iPhone app for people who love cinema. Discover new trailers, what's new in theaters and streaming, keep a living library of everything you've watched and everything you still want to, and never miss a release you've been waiting for. Record not just a star rating but how each film made you feel and share your reviews with friends.

Full: Popkorner is a movie-tracking and social app for iPhone, built for people who love cinema and want more than a spreadsheet of star ratings. At its heart is a simple idea: what a film made you feel matters as much as how many stars you'd give it. Discover new trailers, what's in theaters, and what's streaming from a single Spotlight feed. Build a living Library of what you've watched, what's on your list, and upcoming releases you don't want to miss. Give each film a verdict — Flop, Pop, or Top — and tag the emotion it left you with. Follow friends to see what they're watching, share standout moments. Popkorner is made in France, with a lot of care for craft, motion, and the small details that make an app feel alive.
