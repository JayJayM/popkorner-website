Popkorner

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 24, 2026

Popkorner ("we", "us", or "our") is a movie discovery app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Data We Collect

Data Type What Why
Account info Email address, display name Create and manage your account
Sign in with Apple Apple-provided identity token Authenticate without a password
Profile photo An image you optionally upload Personalize your profile
Reviews & ratings Your Pop/Top/Flop verdict, emotion reactions, and short text reviews Core app functionality
Watchlist Movies you mark as "to watch" or "watched" Track what you want to see
Device motion Accelerometer and gyroscope data (processed on-device only) Interactive 3D card effects; never transmitted to any server
Usage & diagnostics Which screens you open, actions you take (such as tapping a verdict or reaction), performance timings, and crash and error reports Understand how the app is used, fix bugs, and improve performance
Session recordings A replay of your on-screen interactions, with text masked and posters and profile pictures hidden, showing layout, taps, and navigation only. Recorded while you are signed in, unless you turn it off Diagnose usability problems and improve the experience

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect your contacts or address book
  • We do not collect health, financial, or biometric data
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to advertisers
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • Note: Apple may also collect anonymized usage and crash data through its built-in App Analytics system, subject to your device privacy settings.

3. How We Collect Data

  • Directly from you — when you create an account, write a review, upload a photo, or add a movie to your watchlist.
  • Sign in with Apple — Apple provides an identity token and, if you choose, your email address.
  • Device sensors — motion data is read locally on your device for visual effects and is never sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage. Your account data, reviews, and watchlist are stored in Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • TMDB (themoviedb.org) — provides movie metadata (titles, posters, descriptions, release dates). We query TMDB on your behalf; no personal data is sent to TMDB. TMDB Privacy Policy.
  • Datadog (datadoghq.com) — provides analytics, crash and error reporting, and session replay, so we can understand how the app is used, diagnose problems, and improve performance. We identify you to Datadog only by a random account identifier — never your email address or name. Datadog processes this data on our behalf in the United States. Datadog Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising or ad-targeting third-party services.

5. Links to Other Sites

The app may contain links to external websites (such as TMDB movie pages or streaming services). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

6. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Popkorner app:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Display your reviews, ratings, and watchlist
  • Show your profile to you (and, if you share a review card, to recipients)
  • Display your public profile, reviews, and activity to other Popkorner users who follow you or visit your profile
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

Our legal basis for analytics, crash reporting, and session replay is our legitimate interest in maintaining and improving the app. Session recordings mask text and hide posters and profile pictures, so they show how the app is used without capturing what you or anyone else has written. You can turn off analytics or session replay at any time in Settings.

7. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure with the following protections:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security — users can only access their own data
  • Authentication tokens are managed by Supabase Auth
  • Profile photos are stored in access-controlled Supabase Storage buckets

International Data Transfers

Analytics and diagnostics data is processed by Datadog in the United States. Where your personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, that transfer is protected by appropriate safeguards: Datadog is certified under the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework and additionally relies on the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently and irreversibly delete the following from the app, immediately:

  • Your profile and account information
  • All reviews and ratings
  • Your watchlist
  • Your profile photo
  • Your authentication record

Analytics and diagnostics data held by Datadog is keyed to your random account identifier — not your email or name — and is retained for a limited period in line with Datadog’s retention policy. Deleting your account removes your data from the app immediately; you can also stop new analytics collection at any time from Settings.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — view your profile, reviews, and watchlist directly in the app.
  • Delete your data — delete your account from the app (Settings > Account Settings > Delete Account). This permanently removes your data from the app.
  • Withdraw consent — you can stop using the app at any time. Deleting the app alone does not delete your account; use the in-app deletion option first.
  • Opt out of analytics — turn off usage analytics at any time in Settings, under Privacy.
  • Opt out of session replay — turn off session replay at any time in Settings, under Privacy. Recordings mask text and hide posters and profile pictures, and are only made while you are signed in.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you also have the right to request portability of your data and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In France, the relevant authority is the CNIL (cnil.fr). If you are unsatisfied with how we handle your data or a data request, please contact us first at privacy@popkorner.app so we can try to resolve it directly.

For data requests beyond what the app provides, contact us at privacy@popkorner.app.

10. Children's Privacy

Popkorner is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum digital age of consent in your country, if higher). We do not knowingly collect personal information from such children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under the applicable age minimum, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
